**"Case Study: UPI Scam via Screen Sharing**

Type of Crime: The Crime of UPI Fraud

UPI Fraud is an online crime that falls under the category of Cybercrime pertaining to Finance, where the Offender deceives the Victim into making Non-Permitted Transactions or providing Sensitive Financial Data.


Pictorial Representation of the Crime Being Committed (Step by Step):

Bait: The Offender engages the Victim, making an false claim they are either a Bank Employee, KYC Verification Agent or a OLX User.

Hook: The Offender will entice the Victim into the Scam with false Urgency or entice the Victim with a Reward.

Trap: The Offender tricks the Victim into download a “Support Application“ (AnyDesk or TeamViewer). The “Support Application” is a Screen Sharing Application which allows the Offender to access the Victim's Phone.

Theft: The Offender uses the Screen Sharing Application to view the Victims Phone Screen and Will Trick the Victim into entering a UPI PIN on their UPI Application claiming this is a “Test Transaction”.

**Target Audience:**
The Victims of this Scam tend to be University Age and Young Professionals who make on-line purchases on a regular basis."the convenience of third-party applications.

**The Consequences:**
The most important consequence is the loss of money. This loss is immediate and irretrievable if the crime is reported after the "Golden Hour,"which is the first two hours following the commission of the crime.
